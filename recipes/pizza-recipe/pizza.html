Pizza recipe

"Delicious Homemade Pepperoni Pizza"

Pepperoni pizza is a timeless classic, blending zesty flavors and mouthwatering appeal. A perfect harmony of crust, tangy tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella, and abundant sliced pepperoni creates a tantalizing experience. With its spicy, smoky kick, it remains a beloved favorite for all ages and occasions.

The recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough or pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Optional: dried oregano and red pepper flakes for added flavor

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the pizza dough packaging.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the sliced pepperoni on top of the cheese.
  7. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza to enhance the flavors.
  8. Optionally, sprinkle some dried oregano and red pepper flakes for added taste.
  9. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for the time specified on the pizza dough packaging or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a minute or two, slice, and enjoy your delicious homemade pepperoni pizza!
