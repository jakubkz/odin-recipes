Burger recipe

"Ultimate Cheeseburger Recipe"

The cheeseburger, a beloved American favorite, offers a delightful combination of savory beef, melted cheese, and fresh toppings on a soft bun. It's a timeless classic that satisfies cravings and wins hearts with its irresistible charm.

The recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or Swiss)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill or a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into four equal portions and shape them into patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the burger patties on the preheated grill or skillet for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on top of each patty to let it melt slightly.
  5. While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the cheeseburgers by placing a cooked patty on the bottom half of each bun.
  7. Top the patties with lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, and pickles.
  8. Optionally, add ketchup and mustard on the top half of the buns.
  9. Place the top half of the bun over the toppings to complete the cheeseburgers.
  10. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade cheeseburgers!
