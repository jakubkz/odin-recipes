Tomato soup recipe

"Classic Tomato Soup Recipe"

Tomato soup, a timeless comfort food, delights with its velvety texture and harmonious flavors. Whether as an appetizer or main course, it brings comfort and joy, often paired with grilled cheese. A cherished classic for all ages.

The recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz (800g) canned diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, saut√©ing until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.
  2. Pour in the canned diced tomatoes, including their juice, and the vegetable or chicken broth.
  3. Stir in the sugar, dried basil, and dried oregano, then bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  4. Allow the soup to simmer for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. If desired, use an immersion blender or transfer the soup to a blender to puree until smooth. Be cautious when blending hot liquids to avoid splattering.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream (if using) to add creaminess to the soup.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot, garnishing with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  9. Enjoy your delicious homemade tomato soup!
