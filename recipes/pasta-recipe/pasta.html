Pasta recipe(Carbonara)

"Classic Carbonara Recipe"

Carbonara pasta, an Italian treasure, delights with its rich and creamy flavors. Al dente spaghetti, guanciale or pancetta, eggs, Pecorino Romano, and black pepper create a velvety sauce. The smoky meat, sharp cheese, and peppery notes make each bite irresistible. A timeless favorite that captivates food lovers worldwide.

The recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz (340g) spaghetti or linguine
  • 1/2 cup guanciale or pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While the pasta cooks, saut√© the diced guanciale or pancetta in a separate pan over medium heat until it turns crispy and golden.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, immediately add it to the pan with the crispy guanciale or pancetta. Toss to coat the pasta with the rendered fat.
  5. Remove the pan from the heat, and quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing vigorously to create a creamy sauce. The heat from the pasta will cook the eggs, creating a smooth texture.
  6. If the sauce seems too thick, gradually add some of the reserved pasta water until it reaches your desired consistency.
  7. Season with salt if needed (remember that the guanciale or pancetta and cheese are already salty).
  8. Optionally, garnish with chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness.
  9. Serve immediately, and enjoy your delicious homemade Carbonara pasta!
